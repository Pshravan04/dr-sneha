MEET DR. SNEHA

A physician, psychotherapist and healer — holding space for the whole of you.

Dr. Sneha Rooh is a Palliative Physician and Integrative Psychotherapist whose work sits at the intersection of medicine, psychology, somatic healing, creative arts and energy work.

With over six years of clinical and therapeutic experience, she supports individuals navigating grief, trauma, chronic illness, caregiver stress, burnout, body image distress and existential concerns — with care that is trauma-informed, queer-affirmative and neurodivergent-affirmative.

Palliative Physician Integrative Psychotherapist Somatic Therapist Trauma-Informed Practitioner Reiki Master (Level 2)

Healing isn't about fixing what's broken. It's about remembering what's whole — and meeting yourself there with compassion, courage and care.

Her Journey

From palliative medicine to integrative psychotherapy — a path shaped by presence.

Dr. Sneha Rooh began her career as a palliative physician, working alongside individuals and families facing serious illness, loss and end-of-life transitions. In those rooms, she witnessed something profound: that healing is not always about cure, and that the deepest wounds are often carried in silence.

This experience led her to pursue training in integrative psychotherapy, somatic healing, creative arts therapy and energy work — building a practice that bridges the wisdom of medicine with the depth of therapeutic presence.

"Healing lives in the space between expertise and humanity. My work is to hold that space — with reverence, humility and care."

— Dr. Sneha Rooh
